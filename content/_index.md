Guy de Maupassant wrote more than 300 short stories. English readers have access to good translations of a few dozen of them, while many of the rest are either still untranslated or available only in century-old public domain editions, often abridged or bowdlerized{{ sidenote(num="1", body='See my notes <a href="https://myersm0.github.io/maupassant-study/blog/2026-02-intro-to-isosceles-1/#maupassant-mcmaster">here</a>, for example.') }}.

This site documents my attempt to study the stories thoroughly in the original French, to translate them into English — all of them, beginning with some of the lesser-known ones — and to share my findings and the computational [tools](@/tools/_index.md) I've built in the process.
